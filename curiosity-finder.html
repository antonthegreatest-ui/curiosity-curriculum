The Platypus
Unit — a real example

"Platypus" returns zero results on AQA. So a teacher wrote one from scratch. Here's what it looks like.

📋
Unit title: Introduction to the Platypus
1
Identify at least three physical features of a platypus
2
Describe where platypuses live and what they eat
3
Explain one thing that makes platypuses unusual compared to other mammals
4
Find out one threat to platypuses in the wild
5
Share what you've learned — in any way you choose

Five simple outcomes. Any topic. Any student. That's all it takes.
